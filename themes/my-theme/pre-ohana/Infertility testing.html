Infertility Testing

Understanding Fertility Through Accurate Diagnosis

What Is Infertility Testing?

Infertility testing involves medical examinations to identify factors that may affect the ability to conceive. Testing is recommended when pregnancy does not occur after a certain period of trying.

At Keiai Clinic, we perform comprehensive and evidence-based tests for both women and men to guide appropriate treatment.

When Should You Consider Testing?

  • No pregnancy after 12 months of regular attempts
  • No pregnancy after 6 months for women aged 35 or older
  • Irregular or absent menstrual cycles
  • History of miscarriage or gynecological disease
  • Concerns about male fertility

Female Fertility Testing

Hormone Blood Tests

Evaluation of ovarian reserve and hormonal balance, including AMH, FSH, and LH levels.

Ultrasound Examination

Assessment of ovarian follicles and uterine condition using transvaginal ultrasound.

Ovulation Assessment

Confirmation of ovulation timing through hormonal or ultrasound monitoring.

Tubal & Uterine Evaluation

Examination of fallopian tube patency and uterine structure when indicated.

Male Fertility Testing

Semen Analysis

Evaluation of sperm count, motility, morphology, and overall quality.

Hormonal Evaluation

Blood tests to assess hormones related to sperm production.

Medical History Review

Assessment of lifestyle, medical conditions, and prior treatments.

After Testing

Test results are carefully reviewed by our specialists. Based on the findings, we recommend appropriate next steps, which may include lifestyle guidance, medical treatment, or assisted reproductive options.

Clear explanation and shared decision-making are important parts of our diagnostic process.
