Other Treatments and Examinations

Additional Medical Options Supporting Fertility Care

Overview

In addition to standard infertility testing and treatment, Keiai Clinic offers a range of supplementary examinations and therapies to support accurate diagnosis and individualized care.

These options may be recommended depending on medical history, previous treatment outcomes, or specific clinical concerns.

Additional Examinations

Hormonal Evaluation

Detailed blood tests to assess reproductive hormones, thyroid function, and metabolic balance.

Ultrasound Examination

Transvaginal or abdominal ultrasound to evaluate ovarian reserve, uterine shape, and endometrial condition.

Endometrial Assessment

Evaluation of the uterine lining to support embryo implantation planning.

Semen Function Tests

Advanced analysis beyond basic semen testing, including motility and functional evaluation.

Other Treatments

Ovulation Support Therapy

Medication-based support to improve ovulatory function under careful medical monitoring.

Hormonal Regulation

Adjustment of hormonal balance to support menstrual regularity and fertility outcomes.

Endometrial Preparation

Medical management to optimize uterine conditions prior to embryo transfer or conception attempts.

Supportive Medical Care

Treatment of underlying conditions such as anemia, thyroid disorders, or inflammation.

Individualized Treatment Planning

Not all examinations or treatments are required for every patient. Our specialists carefully evaluate each case to recommend only what is medically appropriate.

Clear explanations and shared decision-making are central to our clinical philosophy.
