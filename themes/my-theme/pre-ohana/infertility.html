Infertility

Understanding, Diagnosis, and Treatment

What Is Infertility?

Infertility is generally defined as the inability to conceive after a period of regular, unprotected sexual intercourse. It affects both women and men and may have various causes.

At Keiai Clinic, we emphasize early evaluation, accurate diagnosis, and individualized treatment planning.

When to Seek Medical Advice

  • No pregnancy after 12 months of trying
  • No pregnancy after 6 months for women aged 35 or older
  • Irregular menstrual cycles or ovulation problems
  • History of miscarriage or gynecological disease
  • Concerns regarding male fertility

Common Causes of Infertility

Female Factors

Ovulatory disorders, tubal blockage, uterine abnormalities, endometriosis, and age-related decline.

Male Factors

Low sperm count, reduced motility, abnormal morphology, or hormonal issues.

Combined / Unexplained

Factors involving both partners or infertility with no identifiable cause.
