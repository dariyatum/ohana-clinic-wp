What Is Infertility Treatment?

Infertility treatment includes a range of medical approaches aimed at supporting pregnancy when natural conception does not occur as expected.

At Keiai Clinic, we focus on identifying the underlying causes of infertility and providing individualized treatment plans based on medical findings and patient needs.

Who Should Consider Consultation?

  • Couples unable to conceive after one year of trying
  • Women over 35 who have difficulty becoming pregnant
  • Irregular menstrual cycles or ovulation issues
  • Suspected male factor infertility
  • History of miscarriage or gynecological conditions

※ Early consultation may improve treatment options and outcomes.

Initial Examinations

Hormonal Testing

Blood tests to evaluate ovarian function and hormonal balance.

Ultrasound Examination

Assessment of uterus and ovaries using transvaginal ultrasound.

Semen Analysis

Evaluation of sperm count, motility, and morphology.

Tubal & Uterine Evaluation

Examination of fallopian tubes and uterine structure when needed.

Treatment Options

Timing Guidance

Ovulation monitoring and guidance to optimize natural conception.

Ovulation Induction

Medication to stimulate ovulation when ovulatory disorders are present.

Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)

Placement of prepared sperm into the uterus to increase pregnancy chances.

Advanced Reproductive Treatments

Transition to IVF or other advanced treatments when appropriate.

Important Considerations

Infertility treatment outcomes vary based on age, medical condition, and treatment method. No treatment guarantees pregnancy.

Emotional support and clear communication are essential parts of fertility care.
