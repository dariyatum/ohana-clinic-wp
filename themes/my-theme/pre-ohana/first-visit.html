First Visit Information

Your guide to a smooth and comfortable first appointment

Welcome to Keiai Clinic

We understand that visiting a fertility clinic for the first time can feel overwhelming. Our team is here to support you with compassion, clarity, and professional medical care.

First Visit Flow

01

Make a Reservation

Appointments are required. Please book online or call us before your visit.

02

Reception

Please arrive early and submit your documents at the front desk.

03

Medical Consultation

The doctor will review your history and discuss your concerns.

04

Examination & Tests

Necessary fertility-related tests may be performed on the same day.

05

Explanation & Planning

We will explain results and suggest a personalized treatment plan.

What to Bring

  • Health insurance card (if applicable)
  • Referral letter (if available)
  • Previous medical records
  • Personal identification
  • Medication list

Consultation Time & Fees

Consultation Time

The first consultation usually takes 30–60 minutes depending on your medical background.

Medical Fees

Fees depend on examinations and treatments. We provide clear explanations before proceeding.

Important Notes

  • Please arrive at least 10 minutes before your appointment.
  • Contact us in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule.
  • Certain tests depend on your menstrual cycle.
  • Partners are welcome to attend consultations.
